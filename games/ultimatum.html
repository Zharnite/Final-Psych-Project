A game of a game theory game

Ultimatum

Situation

You are endowed with a sum of money. You are tasked with splitting it with another player, me. Once you communicate your decision, I may accept it or reject it. If the I accept, the money is split per the proposal; if I reject, we both receive nothing.

You have $100. How much do you give me?
