A game of a game theory game

Prisoner's Dilemma

The Situation

You and your best friend robbed the president of the US. Now you and your friend are being interogated by the federal government.

You are given a deal. If you don't speak and your friend don't speak, both of you are only given 5 years. If both of you speak, you are given 20 years. However, if one of you speaks, the one who speaks is only given 2 years and the other is given 30 years.

Betry your friend or no?
