Blotto Game

The Battlefield

Players are tasked to simultaneously distribute limited troops on the battlefield. In the classic version of the game, the player devoting the most resources to a battlefield wins that battlefield, and the gain (or payoff) is then equal to the total number of battlefields won.

In this game, you have 25 soldiers you need to distribute on 5 territories.
